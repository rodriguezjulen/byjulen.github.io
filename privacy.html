Privacy Policy (GeoFlags)

Controller

GeoFlags (educational app; no account registration required).

Data We Collect

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information.
  • We store locally on your device: quiz history, statistics (score, streak, accuracy), settings (theme, language, sounds), and level/XP progress.
  • We send basic app events (e.g., installation and app open) to analytics providers for measurement and attribution: Firebase Analytics (GA4, without Ad ID) and/or TikTok App Events SDK. These providers may receive device identifiers (e.g., IDFV) and technical data (app version, device model, approximate IP), depending on system settings.

Purpose of Processing

  • Provide app functionality (show progress, achievements, personalize the experience).
  • Measure basic app performance and installs for campaign attribution.

Legal Basis

Legitimate interest and/or consent (e.g., ATT in iOS), depending on your jurisdiction and system settings.

Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you choose to delete it (by clearing history in the app or uninstalling the app).
  • Analytics data is managed by providers according to their policies.

Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your data with third parties except for the basic events sent to the analytics providers mentioned above, in accordance with their privacy policies.

Analytics and Advertising

Basic usage/install analytics only. We do not display ads in the app and do not engage in cross-app or cross-site tracking.

Your Controls

  • You can limit tracking in iOS via Settings > Privacy > Tracking (ATT).
  • You can clear your history in the app or uninstall it to remove all local information.

Children's Privacy

GeoFlags is educational and may be used by minors under parental supervision. We do not collect personal data or store sensitive information about children.

International Transfers

Analytics providers may process data outside your country (e.g., in the United States or elsewhere). See the privacy policies of Firebase and TikTok for details.

Security

Local information is stored using system mechanisms (iOS UserDefaults). Providers apply their own security measures.

Your Rights

You can manage or delete your local data at any time within the app. You can also uninstall the app to remove all stored information.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or changes in the app. The updated version will be published in the app.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact the developer through the app's App Store listing.

Note: This policy is also available within the app under Settings > Legal > Privacy Policy.
